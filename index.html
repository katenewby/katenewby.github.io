How to house hunt with success!

Finding a House

Use Rightmove Students to search for properties advertised by landlords and letting agents.
Use the UoB bulletin board or this facebook page to find people looking for housemates.
Contact the univeristy accommodation office about returning to halls of residence or apply to become a senior resident.

Advice

  • Don't panic! Houses are released in batches; November/December, and January/February. If you don't get a house before christmas, there is still time in the new year.
  • To avoid conflict pick housemates you match well with! Budget, noisiness, tidiness, and heating requirements are important!
  • Location, location location! Decide how long a walk (or bus journey) you are willing to take every day to campus! Houses further out may be cheaper, but is that worth the queue for a bus, or a 50 minute walk in the rain?
